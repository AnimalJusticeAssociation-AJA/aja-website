Our Services

What we do here at AJA

Education

We educate the public on the different forms of animal abuse, how they happen, and how they can be solved. Our classes are designed for all age groups and we dedicate ourselves to teaching with passion and factual knowledge.

Events

We hold monthly events with the public in partnership with other animal conservation organizations to undertake hands-on research and collect data to input into our machine learning and data science models

Research Articles

Here at AJA, we write research articles where we inform the general public on the different circumstances of abuse. Our articles include evidence on how much animal abuse occurs, where, and the diffrent reasons why it happens.

About Us

We are the
Animal Justice Association

Our organization strives to give animals facing abuse, due to humanâ€™s constant desire for entertainment, a second chance. We achieve our goals of offering a new life for tortured animals by raising public awareness. Our mission is to raise awareness of the particular instances that occur behind the scenes of highly attractive amusement parks that feature animals as their main attractions. We hope to put an end to animal abuse, because animals deserve better! We will hold a wide array of events which the public can participate in, including fundraisers, petition events, speaker events, and many more. Our organization serves justice to these anguished animals by ending businesses that profit off the act of admiring caged animals and try our very best to send these animals to the wild.
